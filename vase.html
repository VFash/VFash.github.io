Vase Body Shape

Elegance

What is the Vase body shape?

Vase bodies similar to the hourglass but the difference it's stretched. Often recognized as one of the most elegant shapes.Major features of the vase figure include big bust, gently curving enlongated waists, equal waist, and with slimmer waist.

What to Wear

When picking for this shape think classic and elegant. Focus on emphasizing you waistline with jackets and tops that fit around your middle."The Classic Hollywood Glamour" shapes and cuts keep embracing those curves

Avoid

Ruffles especially around your neck
Poloneck Jumpers
Princess Collars
Round-neck tops
Dresses /or skirts with frills
Clumpy Heels

Try

Fitted Shirts with puffy sleeves
Three quater length sleeves
Rounded earrings
Curvy shoes
straight jeans or pants
Figure hugging dresses

Styles for your type

Going for a Business? look try trousers with slanted pockets they show off your great legs.To finish it pair with a single button jacket that fits you waist. This helps in showing you feminine shape

High waisted pencil skirts should become your best friend. Pencil skirts take you extended waist and rounds your hips.Try curvy shoes to mimic your curves the slender the heel the better because they go great with you slim legs.

Going for a more casual look? Straight leg jeans fit your shape the best it will fall from your hips , but still help in show the slenderness of you leg. Square neckline tops will show off your shape without giving too much cleavage. Play with colors and patterns but stray away from frills or anything that gathers at your frame.
