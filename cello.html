Cello Body Shape

Dare to Show your curves

What is the Cello Body Type?

As a cello you have a curvy body type, embrace it. The cello is very close to an hourglass. Cello's have a short waist but not as small and a fuller figure. You may feel more on the pear shape spectrum, however broad shoulders and big bust balance it out. Cello's have lartge boobs, short waist, big hips, big thighs,broad shoulders, and slim lower legs. For a cello, it's all about daring to show yourselves off

What to Wear

As a cello, you have what it takes to pull off almost anything in boldness, strong prints and fussy details that would swamp other body types. Full length wide-leg trousers are great pants for you to wear comfortably. When wearing skinny jeans, use loose and forgiving tops to make sure the cut doesn't end on your widest part. Crotch to mid thigh are ideal lengths. Tulip tops are very forgiving tops. Keep your bottom half simple, too much on the bottom will draw unwanted attention. Pencil skirts don't flatter your shape ut figure hugging dress can rock your image. Firming effects and thick material with gentle folds and tummy covering function will be your best dress friend. Try Halter neck dresses and wide waist belts. Remember you can wear bold, display your attributes up too.
