What is the Hourglass body shape?

Hourglass bodies have the curves everyone adores. With large boobs, a small and short waist, big hips and generous thighs can make finding clothes hard to find. Just realize you havenothing to hide and to accentuate everything you have.

What to Wear

Classic lines and styles are your secret weapon. Such as V neck knits and pnecil skirts. Elastic material will shape your body. Make sure your clothing insn't too wide one place and too small somewhere else. Wear clothes that lift your breasts and elongate your waist. Think 50's glamour and dare to show your curves! Don't cover up in bulky clothing unless you do it right. Wear only one oversized item at a time. Pants are hare to find for hourglass shapes. Try wearing drapery wide legged pants or high waisted skinny jeans. Just remember showing your figure is different than showing skin. Embrace your curves!
