Pear Body Shape

You are unique as being part of 60% of the UK ages 18-30

What is the Pear Shape?

The idea that the pear shape is viewed as overweight can be completely untrue. Most Likely you are a pear body shape simply because your thighs and bottom half are a bit bigger than your bust and top half. As a pear you have good things going forn you on your top half so focus on that when you are dressing for the day. Your back, boods and arms are almost all winners with this body shape.

The classic pear is a long waist, flat tummy, great arms and heavy legs.

What to wear?

Cropped pants are not your friend. They will draw the eye to your legs in the wrong way, however trying wide-legged trousers with a flat front will accent your shape in all the right places.b Be sure to balance everything below the waist, wear large earrings and eye catching necklaces. Skin colored heels and wedges will do your legs justice!
