Cornet Body Type

You are a 90's Super Model

What is the Cornet Body Shape?

Cornet bodies are often described as athletic or "Tom Boy" Bodies. Cornets resemble an upside down triangle with broad shoulders and slim hips.

What to Wear

The best advice is to stay away from anything that will add to your shoulder width. Three Quartered sleeves and oversized arm sleeves will make you look masculine. Diamond necklines are great for reducing your shoulder width. Draping clothes is easily forgiving and break up your shape by adding bold patterns and bright colors. Skinny jeans are piratically made for you because of your long and slender legs. Wear slim and shapely heels, too chunky platforms look overwhelming.

Avoid

Anything shapeless from the shoulders
Floaty and shapeless dresses
Scooped necklines
Cut away shoulders
Shoulder Pads... You aren't a foot ball player don't do it...
Vertical designs on the shoulder
Turtle necks
Platform shoes
Three Quarter Sleeves

Try

Broad Straps on the shoulders
Big collars and wide lapels on fitted shirts,blazers and jackets
Single button coat with a full skirt
Wide diamond and V-shape neck opening
Wrapped knitwear and tops that nip the waist
Asymmetrical wrap cardigan
Asymmetric and draped dresses
Flared and fluted skirts(Both Maxi and Mini)
Pencil skirts
Cigarette Pants and skinny jeans
Low leg swimsuit
Slim Heeled Shoes
