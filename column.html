The Straight and Slender

Love Your Shape

Column shape is known as the shoulder witdh being the same as hip. You have a slim waist and long legs , and you can the most out of your column shape by making creating curves your focus. Try choosing clothing items with details such as embellishments.

Try to Avoid...
  • Clothing that is tight on your figure.
  • Straight fitted lines in dresses , trousers, and sleeves.
  • Tops/ or dresses with square necklines..
  • Shapeless jackets, loose or droopy styles that hang from your shoulders
  • Drooped waistlines.
  • Bulky Textures.

    • But don't be discouraged..

    There are plenty of styles that work for your shape. The column shape was destined for rocking the "Boyfriend Look".Rounder cuts are great to help in giving you a extra curve to prevent looking too straight up and down. Accessories are your best friend keep them big and bold.

    What to Wear

  • Ruffles , cowl, collars, necktie,they give roundness and put focus on centre body.

  • Jackets that are belted and that details. Try jackets with pockets and shoudler details such wide lapels
  • Chose dresses that princess line with curved darts deatils, wrap, empire line.
  • skinny and or bootcut leg jeans.
