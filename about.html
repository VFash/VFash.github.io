Who we are

V Fash is created and run by four high-school girls who wanted to change the way people feel about their body image and the way they online shop. The four of us met through Girls Who Code a non-profit organization specializing in teaching young high-school age girls how to code in various programming languages and closing the gender gap in the technology industry. After meeting through Girls Who Code, we created V Fash as our final project and plan on taking it to the next level by creating an online shopping center based on the way each person is individually shaped.
