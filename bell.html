What is the Bell Body Shape?

The Bell body shape has a small top half and larger bottom half. At a young age you were a small lady with a nice round bottom but as years advance, so does your tush.

What to Wear

You shouldn't try to cover up your beautiful self so to feel more comfortable in your skin based on the shape of your outside shell, we have some dos and do nots.

The easiest way to balance your top and bottom halves is to subtly make your top half more noticeable. Add details and colors on top and plainness to the bottom so that your thighs and tush don't draw the unwanted attention.

Avoid

Wide V-Necks clothes
Skimpy Vests
Anything elaborate or fussy on your bottom half

Try

Cowl Neck Jumpers
Flowing trousers
A line Dresses
Funnel coats
Eye catching necklaces

Stylish Outfits and Accessories
