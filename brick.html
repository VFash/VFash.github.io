Brick

The Powerful and Fierce

Similar to the Column your shape is also straight up and down, but different from the Column You are slightly stockier. Don't sweat Brick is great for a shapely outfit

Steer away from..
  • Boxy jackets are to echo the straight up and down of your shape.
  • Strapless tops/ or dresses help in broadening the shoulders.
  • Mini Skirts rectangle bottoms makes the rest your body look rectangular too
  • Double breasted jackets
  • Heavy wedge shoes. curvy shoes are best for you.

    • Gears for Brick

  • Something that creates a waist, such as tightly belted jackets.
  • Round neckline top
  • Graphic print and decoration.
  • Gently draping jersey tops.
  • Tops with a pattern to break up the larger body area.
  • High-waisted wrap dress.
  • Asymmetrical and panelled skirts that drape nicely over your hips and creates shape.
  • Loose but not too big trousers.
  • Curvy shoes !!!

    • Work these

    Fitted Tank

    A fitted tank top or vest is a Bricks best friend.Find one with a slight curve in it and use it over other tops to cling to your frame .

    Fitted Knit

    A knit will cling to your body and with the right detailing will help to create the waist that you need. This particular top will open up your chest and the banding at the waist effectively draws a waist-line in.

    High Wrapped Dress

    Dress is great for anyone except those with very small breasts and particularly so for the Brick.
